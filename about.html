About ArtFunFest Afro Fusion

Celebrating African Art & Culture in Ago Iwoye

Our Story

Art Funfest Afro Fusion began as OOU Ibogun Art Funfest, a bold initiative at Olabisi Onabanjo University (Ibogun Campus). Founded by Daodu Samuel, then Social Director of the Fine and Applied Arts Department, with support from OOU SUG VP4 Sen. Mhidex, the festival addressed a key challenge: talented fine artists lacked platforms to showcase their work, often traveling to cities like Ago-Iwoye for exposure.

Art Funfest changed that by uniting audiences, artists, and creatives from various campuses to celebrate art in Ibogun. Despite challenges like transportation, poor roads, limited sponsorship, and distance, it grew into a vibrant hub for creativity.

Today, OOU Ibogun Art Funfest has evolved into Art Funfest Afro Fusion – a cultural festival bringing African art and culture to a global audience, starting from Ogun State.

"Art Funfest Afro Fusion is a movement that empowers artists and celebrates the raw beauty of African creativity."
— Daodu Samuel, Founder
