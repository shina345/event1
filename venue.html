Festival Venue

Central Park - The heart of New York's cultural scene

Location

Central Park, New York

Experience ArtFunFest in one of the world's most iconic urban parks

ArtFunFest 2025 takes place in the magnificent Central Park, spanning across multiple scenic locations within this 843-acre oasis in the heart of Manhattan. Our festival transforms the park's natural beauty into a stunning backdrop for three days of art, music, and cultural celebration.

The venue features multiple stages, exhibition spaces, and interactive installations strategically placed throughout the park's most picturesque areas, including the Great Lawn, Bethesda Fountain, and the Mall.
